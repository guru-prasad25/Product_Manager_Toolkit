# Product Manager's Toolkit 🚀

Welcome to my Product Management Toolkit! This repository is a collection of frameworks, templates, and real-world insights from my experience as a Product Manager across different domains including fintech, gaming, mobility, and B2B SaaS.

## What's Inside

### 📊 Data-Driven Decision Making
- Frameworks for implementing product analytics (inspired by my work at MPL building cross-platform dashboards)
- Templates for A/B testing and experiment documentation
- Churn prediction models and user retention analysis frameworks
- Sample KPI definitions and measurement approaches
- Digital adoption measurement templates

### 🎯 User Onboarding & Activation
- User onboarding optimization playbook (based on experience increasing activation rates at Smartlead)
- Digital adoption implementation guides
- User feedback collection frameworks
- Activation metrics measurement templates
- Examples of successful onboarding flows

### 🛠 Product Development & Infrastructure
- Templates for multi-tenant infrastructure planning
- Safety protocols for critical releases
- Feature parity analysis frameworks
- Product telemetry audit systems
- Integration planning templates (drawn from experience with insurance provider integrations)

### 📈 Growth & Engagement
- Client referral program frameworks
- User engagement measurement templates
- Geolocation feature implementation guides
- International expansion playbooks
- Partner retention strategies

### 📝 Process Templates
- Sprint planning and backlog grooming guides
- Cross-functional collaboration frameworks
- Product roadmap templates
- Feature request collection systems
- User feedback prioritization frameworks

## Who This Is For

This toolkit is designed for:
- Product Managers looking for practical, battle-tested templates
- Teams seeking to implement data-driven product development
- Startups building scalable product processes
- Anyone interested in modern product management practices

## How to Use This Repository

1. Browse through the directories based on your current needs
2. Each template includes:
   - Context and when to use it
   - Implementation guidelines
   - Real-world examples (anonymized)
   - Lessons learned
   - Customization tips
